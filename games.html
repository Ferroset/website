Dodge-Grays

Navigate through a field of dangerous particles! Use arrow keys or on-screen controls to avoid the red particles while collecting green power-ups. Can you survive and achieve the highest score?

Score

How to Play

  • Desktop: Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen D-pad controls
  • Avoid: Red particles (they hurt!)
  • Collect: Green power-ups for bonuses
  • Survive: As long as possible and rack up points
  • Level Up: Every 1000 points increases difficulty
